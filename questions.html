By different measures of certainty, this study will assess your willingness to accept the representation of a product with a given composition of properties. In a moment, you will be asked to make judgments about various products. On the screen, you will be shown an image of a product. You will then be asked to judge and rate the product in terms of surprise, ease-of-imagining, goodness-of-example, and similarity-to-an-ideal on a scale from 1-7. For example, for the product ‘house’ with the feature ‘has a roof’, you would be shown an image of a house that has a roof. Then, if asked to make a judgment about surprise you would be asked, “How surprised would you be to encounter a house that has a roof?” You would, then, respond by rating your level of surprise on a scale from 1-7.
